1.1. Meeting on <2023-01-29 Sun>

The overall conclusion from the meeting is that there will be three tracks:

  1. Hack-the-box
  2. Capture the Flag
  3. Security by Design

Track three will be devoted to DIH. The goal would be to strenghten an existing codebase and improve the security of an application for the medical sector in BiH.

The competition will be held over three days. First day will host the first two tracks, wile the secod day will be devoted to the track three.
